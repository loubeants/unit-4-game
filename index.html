Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game, which will display under 'Targer Score'
there are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of a point to 'your score'.
you win the game by matching your score to the target score, you lose the game if your score goes above the target Score.
the value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal, good luck.
